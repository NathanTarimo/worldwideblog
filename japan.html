HOW TO GET THERE?

In order to get to Japan from Dubai, you must book a flight. After that, keep tabs on the assigned time and day you are supposed to be at your flight so that you will not have to worry about missing your flight. While you are preparing for your flight, make sure you have all the belongings you will need which will include your passport, Visa (if required), and any health documents.
WHEN TO GO THERE?

I would reccomend for you to fly from Dubai to Japan during the winter holidays.
